Introduction

Llama is a camelidae animal, originates from South Ameriaca. However, it has been domesticated for thousands of years. So nowadays llamas can be found everywhere over the world.

It is mainly used for transportation because it is a strongly durable animal. In addition, it is a social animal that llamas always live together as herds. Llamas' ears are "Banana" shaped. They are long and slightly curved inward.
